CHOCOLATE SHAKE

Satisfy your chocolate cravings with this indulgent Chocolate Milkshake, a creamy, dreamy delight that’s sure to please your taste buds with every sip. Made with just five simple ingredients, rich cocoa powder, chilled milk, warm water, ice cubes, and the irresistible sweetness of Nestlé MILKMAID, this refreshing treat is perfect whenever you need a sweet, cool pick-me-up. The best part? This chocolate shake recipe is ready in just minutes! Simply blend cocoa powder, chilled milk, warm water, ice cubes, and a generous drizzle of Nestlé MILKMAID until smooth and creamy. Pour it into a glass and top it with whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or a maraschino cherry for that irresistible finishing touch.

INGREDIENTS

  • Nestle MILKMAID Mini
  • Nestle A+ Milk
  • Cocoa powder
  • Warm water
  • Ice cubes

STEPS

  1. Mix cocoa powder with warm water till no lumps.
  2. Add the cocoa mixture and the rest of the ingredients to a mixer jar and blend till smooth.
  3. Transfer to a tall glass.
  4. Serve chilled.
